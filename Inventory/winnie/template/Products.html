Product Details

The Excel data details a product inventory, classifying items into Finished Products (FERT), Raw Materials (ROH), and more. Finished Products include Nut Muesli and Blueberry Muesli, each in 500g and 1kg sizes. Raw Materials encompass packaging options (Large Box, Small Bag) and individual ingredients such as Nuts, Blueberries, and Oats, all in 1kg units. The Material Code uniquely identifies each item, and the Type Code indicates its category. This organized dataset facilitates inventory management, outlining product types, quantities, and categorization for efficient production tracking and procurement of external materials.
